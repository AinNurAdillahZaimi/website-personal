Primary School

- SK Ramuan China Besar & SRAJ Ramuan China Besar -
[2007-2013]

I entered my primary school in 2007 and ended up in 2013. During my childhood, I made so many friends. It has been 9 years since I left the school, but all the memories are still fresh in my mind. I will never forget all the teachers who have taught me, especially Cikgu Zawiah, Cikgu Chesu and other teachers. Without them, I will never come successfully now. I still remember the day I got beaten by my English teacher for not achieving the KPI for English paper one. It is just a nightmare for all students including me. All of the memories will be kept forever and ever.

Secondary School

- SBPI Selandar -
[2014-2018]

I got into a boarding school in 2013. Actually, before applying to my secondary school I never thought that I would be living independently alone in the hostel. By entering boarding school, it has taught me much moral value. For example, taught me to manage my time wisely, be responsible for all my actions and always respect people around us. Not just that, throughout 5 years. I am also grateful to meet such a wonderful best friend at school. We shared lots of tears, stress, and happiness together.

University
